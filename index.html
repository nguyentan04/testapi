NFT MARKET
A pair of shoes is an indispensable item for your activities.
You can search for shoes with high attributes or resell them through the NFT MarketPlace.
SHOE COLLECTION
Each pair of shoes includes different attributes.
We bring you 3 types of shoes with different limits according to each type.
What's more, these attributes can be upgraded according to different levels.
TOKEN MECHANIC
